Recommender Systems

Recommender systems are used by many different websites to better consumer experience.

Types of Recommender Systems

Recommender systems are broadly classified in to content-based systems and collaborative filtering systems.

Collaborative Filtering

Collaborative filtering uses information about the user and his/her relationship to the item.

Item-based collaborative filtering

The utility function is a function of the user's preference for the item multiplied by the similarity of the item to another item that the user liked before.
